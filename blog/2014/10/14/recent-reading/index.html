About Me

I'm the founder & Chief Person of Ludometrics, a games company based in Glasgow, Scotland. We're making Bodycheck for PS Vita. We also made Bips! for Facebook, Gridlock for PC, Mac, OUYA and GameStick. And for iOS: Dsmvwld, Matching Set and Sock Monster. Prior to starting Ludometrics, I worked at Denki, Slam and The Games Kitchen. I've been making games for mobile devices since 1999 (on and off) and for everything else since forever.

There's no particular topic for this blog, other than what's on my mind. Which is usually something games-related.
